About This Journal

Welcome to my personal cybersecurity journal where I document my journey through the ever-evolving landscape of information security. This space serves as both a knowledge repository and a reflection of my professional growth in cybersecurity.

Here you'll find technical write-ups, vulnerability research, defensive strategies, and occasional philosophical musings about security in our digital age. All content represents my personal views and experiences.

Security Focus Areas

  • Methodical security assessments identifying vulnerabilities in systems, networks, and applications through simulated attacks.

  • Analysis of emerging threats, attacker TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures), and proactive defense strategies.

  • Integrating security throughout the SDLC to build resilient applications from the ground up.

  • Strategies and lessons learned from detecting, analyzing, and responding to security incidents.
